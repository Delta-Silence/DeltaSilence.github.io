PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: September 15, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Delta Silence, the creator of the game "Sights of Iron," ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information from users ("you" or "your") of our game.

We are committed to respecting and protecting your privacy. We believe that your personal data should be your own. This policy outlines our strict approach to data collection and use, ensuring a secure and enjoyable gaming experience without compromising your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

In order to provide and enhance the multiplayer experience of Sights of Iron, we collect a limited amount of information directly related to your in-game activity. This information is collected solely through your use of the game's core features:

Text Chat Data: We collect text messages sent through in-game chat to facilitate communication between players and for moderation purposes (e.g., to prevent and address harassment, abuse, or cheating).

Voice Chat Data: We process voice chat data in real-time to enable communication between players. We do not store or record this data. It is used exclusively to transmit your voice to other players during gameplay.

Multiplayer Data: We collect information required for the basic functionality of the game's multiplayer system, such as your in-game username, unique account ID, match history, and performance statistics (e.g., kills, deaths, wins). This data is necessary to provide leaderboards, matchmaking, and a persistent gameplay experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used exclusively for the following purposes:

To operate, maintain, and improve the gameplay and features of Sights of Iron.

To enable multiplayer functionality, matchmaking, and social features.

To moderate in-game conduct, prevent cheating, and enforce our terms of service to ensure a fair and secure environment for all players.

To troubleshoot technical issues and provide customer support.

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We will not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your personal information with any third-party companies, advertisers, or other entities. Your data is handled entirely by Delta Silence. Our commitment to your privacy means we do not engage in any form of data brokerage or commercial use of your information outside of what is necessary to run the game.

4. Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your information. All data transmitted between your device and our servers is secured using encryption. Our servers are protected by firewalls, and access to any collected data is restricted to authorized personnel who need the information to perform their duties.

5. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal data. Because we only collect minimal data, this process is straightforward. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

6. Children's Privacy

Sights of Iron is a game not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately, and we will take steps to remove such information from our systems.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will notify you by posting the new Privacy Policy within the game or on our website. Your continued use of Sights of Iron after any changes signifies your acceptance of the updated policy.
